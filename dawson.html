Dawson Tire and Wheel

A freelance side project to deliver a section of e-commerce

Description

I worked as a freelance UX designer for two months from March 2020 to April 2020 to deliver a section of an e-commerce platform where users can select their desired tires and check the depth of the tire treads.
**Some the designs and branding has been changed, for further details please contact me.**

  • Tools Used: LucidCharts | FIgma | Zeplin
Tire Depth Determination

    Dawson application is designed to record the brand, type of vehicle with the tire inches as well. This information is used to determine the depth of the thread of the tires. User can either record the tires details by taking a photograph of the tire or by typing the information.
