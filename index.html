About Me

Hi guys, I am Abhishek Mhatre, a zealot UI/UX designer with a good user research background. I have helped teams in college by redesigning and improving applications.

I specialize in UX research, UX design, UI design, interactive design, product development. I have done projects such as Slackless Apps, parking lot designs where we had to design the application from the scratch. On a contrary I have done projects such as Linkedin UI re-design, Dil Mil app re-design I had to change the UX and UI designs of the apps as per user suggestions with UCD (user center design approach). I have done a project in Usability testing where we have conducted heuristic evaluations, recorded interviews of participants and submitted the report back to the company.

I have also worked in front end development to design a online application, I have used HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JQuery, javaScript frameworks in these projects. I have also done projects in internet of things, to create ambient displays, artifacts front end designs to display live APIs.

UX Research: I focus on people's behavior, experiences and emotions towards a product as it helps me understand the users and align my design as per expectations.

Ideation: I clearly define the problem and generate multiple solutions to solve a problem.

Prototyping: I create low/high fidelity prototype to bring the idea into existence and test the idea of the product design.

Visual Design: I visualize the information into an engaging UI which will not only be satisfying but a delightful experience to the users.

Front End Development: I like to code the designs which in return helps me my visions, understand technical complexities and bring out new different designs.

Usability Testing: I conduct usability testing to find out problems that might have gone unnoticed. Do quantitative and qualitative analysis generated from participants data and aim to solve those problems for user satisfaction.
